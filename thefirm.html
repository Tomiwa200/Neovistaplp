ABOUT NEOVISTA PRACTICE

Neo Vista Practice LP (“NVPLP”) is a full-service commercial law firm providing a range of legal representation and strategic advice to a diverse client base with offices in Abuja, Nigeria and representative offices in Lagos, Nigeria and London, England. Our firm is peopled by seasoned professionals who are dedicated, vibrant, knowledgeable, innovative and tech-savvy. We pride ourselves as the go-to law firm owing to our dedicated team of lawyers who are profoundly knowledgeable in different aspects of the law, particularly, in commercial litigation, arbitration and transactions advisory. Our lawyers‚ mastery of contemporary legal issues; in-depth understanding of the Nigerian legal landscape and business environment; our signature attention to detail in every matter we undertake; the robust legal representation we offer our clients before the Nigerian Courts and the cutting edge legal and transactions advisory we offer our clients, all stands our firm in good stead.

Our expertise cut across different practice areas namely Corporate and Commercial, Oil and Gas, Natural Resources and Extractive Industries, Litigation and Dispute Resolution, Employment and Labour, Banking and Finance, Admiralty and Maritime, Power and Infrastructure, Real Estate and Construction, Technology, Media and Telecommunications (TMT), Aviation and Aerospace, Sports and Entertainment, Venture Capital and Project Finance, Business Advisory, and Company Secretarial Services. These practice groups are led by lawyers who are respected sector experts.

Our firm has been involved in landmark litigations and we have also provided reliable transaction advisory to major corporations at both national and cross-border levels. We have acted for and advised big private sector companies in virtually all the sectors of the Nigerian economy. We have also advised and represented agencies of the Federal Government of Nigeria/state owned entities and sub-national governments on regulatory and compliance issues. We have built robust relationships with some of the highly respected law firms in various jurisdictions making it easier and more convenient for us to serve our clients global.
